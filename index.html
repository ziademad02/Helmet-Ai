About Helmet AI

Helmet AI is an advanced smart safety system designed to protect workers in hazardous environments. By combining artificial intelligence, smart sensors, and augmented reality technology, the helmet can detect potential dangers such as fire, toxic gases, and obstacles in real time. Helmet AI helps workers stay aware of their surroundings and receive instant alerts.
